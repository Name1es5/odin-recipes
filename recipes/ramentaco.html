Ramen Taco

Description

It's ramen... in a taco. As featured in Cathy Mitchell's award winning cookbook "Ramen Joy".

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Ramen
  • Red Pepper
  • Shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1/2 pound flank steak
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  2. Heat up a medium sized pan, add oil and saute onions, red peppers, salt and pepper.Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. While vegetables are cooking, cook ramen noodles. Add marinated meatand cook for 4 minutes (will depend on the size of the meat).
  4. When noodles are done cooking,drain out water and add the rest of the soup base and seasoning oil.
  5. Mix, set aside. Heat uptortillas on medium high heat and add cheese, coverand let it melt.
  6. Remove from the pan and add vegetables, meat, ramen and lettuce.
